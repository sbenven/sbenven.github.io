Features

Easy to Use

Fill a SolarBag® with 3 liters of fresh water from any source t hrough its included pre-filter. Then place the SolarBag in direct sunlight or in any area open to the sky. The water is purified in 2-3 hours on a sunny day or 4-6 hours on a cloudy day or if the source water is tea-colo red. A Pur-Blue process timer indicates that the purification process is complete. Drink the great tasting, puri fied water.

- It will not clog like a filter and no pumping or backwashing is needed.

- You can use it 100s of times.

- It uses no chemicals so the water tastes great.
