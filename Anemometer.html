Anemometer

WGT-10


The WGT-10 is a JIS T8202 compliant hot-wire anemometer for general use. The integrated automatic thermal compensation maintains the wire at a fixed temperature, allowing stable and accurate readings of the velocity regardless of the air stream temperature. The device is designed to fit the palm of your hand and is easy to operate. This anemometer is perfect for clean room and laboratory use.

Features

  • Displays 3 types of average values - The display switches between real-time, 10-second running average, 30-second average, and 60-second average readings.
  • Detatchable Compatible Probe - Probe compatibility allows you to replace probe only.
  • Backlit LCD Display - Easy-to-read large LCD display with display value of 0.01m/s or 0.1m/s.
  • USB cable - Measured data can be downloaded using included USB cable. The USB cable can also be used as a power cable.
  • Data Management Software - Usability is improved by dedicated software included.
