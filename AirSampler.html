Air Sampler

HSP-10

Portable sampling pump captures particulate matter anywhere.
This portable sampling device captures asbestos, odors, and particulate matters in atmospheric and the working environment. Real-time flow rate and cumulative flow rate can be displayed. With built-in barometer and thermometer, air flow control is compensated. Sampling is possible with timer function and cumulative flow method.

Features

  • Wide range - Built-in MEMS sensor allows constant flow control.
  • Durable waterproof case - Waterproof case and cover protect sensor from external impact or rainwater.
  • High capacity Ni-MH battery - High capacity Ni-MH battery allows more than 9 hours of operation.
  • Data communication - Save log data to USB port.
