Sampling Pump

HSP-500/1500/5000

High durability under high loading conditions.
The Sampling Pump HSP series is a lineup of handy air samplers covering a wide range of flow rates. These pumps are perfect for laboratory use where drawing a carefully controlled volume of air is needed. They are also suited for portable use with belt holders or a shoulder case to monitor working environments.

Features
 Specification Buy (AXEL shop)

Features

  • Built-in Battery - Built-in lithium-ion battery and an attached AC adapter allow easy charge.
  • High Pressure Tolerance - Tolerate high pressure over 12kPa. It operates normally even under high load conditions.
  • Down Timer and Volume Timer Modes - The down timer mode controls pump operation time, and the volume timer mode controls total suction volume.
  • Long time continuous operation time - Over 8 hours of continuous operation with built-in battery.

Convenient symmetric design

Simple & easy to replace filters
